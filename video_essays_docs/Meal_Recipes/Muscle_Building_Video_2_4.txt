Step 7 - Food choices
The importance of the high protein, low fat food choices 
Consuming high quality food that is high protein and low fat daily is the most important key knowledge for bodybuilders. You must have a high protein intake for muscle growth and repair; low fat intake for a lean body definition and a six pack; and lower calories than you need to lose fat.
Practically, foods that you MUST consume regularly to achieve an optimal body shape are: skinless chicken breast; skinless chicken thigh; 5% beef mince; and low fat fish. They are sources of macronutrients that are low calories, high protein and low fat.
